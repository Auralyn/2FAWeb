Why you Need 2-Factor Authorization New

Everywhere online you see forums say "Activate 2-Factor Authorization!" and that makes people wonder : "Why? My password was enough for the past years why not now?"

To answer this question we have to think of following Things : Hackers are evolving and learning. Just a few years ago Sony Pictures Animation was hacked by the North Korean government over a movie. Some passwords they had were literally things like "sonypictures" which is not really secure.

before we start on what 2-FA is we should first concentrate on what makes out a good password because without a good password 2FA won't help.

What makes out a good password?

"password" is the most used password in the world. not kidding. Maybe people just have many throwaway accounts and not bother about the password but that's still frightening. What do you think is a safe password? "MyFirstChildsName69"? "FavouriteFootballTeam"? no. All of those aren't really strong and only require some research to find out.

The best possible solution for a password is simple :

"pz)SfDnhtaA,xMqYdL3s"

. Try to say that out loud! It's hard to remember but that's a good thing. Noone can steal it from you if you dont know it yourself!

What security measures are there?

There are 3 Ways of Authenticating that it is really you logging in an account. Knowledge, which is your password. Belongnings, which is your mobile. And last but not least, biometric data. (e.g. Face Id)

2-Factor Authenticating uses Knowledge and Belongnings and you might realize now why it's so important to have 2Fa activated. Since you need your phone to enable a log-in it prevents people logging in from a completely different state with solely your password.

To conclude Things you should always use 2FA where possible since you never know who might hack into your account! Don't forget your passwords!
